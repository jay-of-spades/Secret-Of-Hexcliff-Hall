Today is the day you inherited that key fob. You have no idea why of all his nieces and nephews, Uncle Matt chose you. But he did. You've wandered for quite a while, trying to find the road that would lead to the estate. It wasn't that difficult... not at all. You simply had to accept that the only way through was by taking the dirt road, then the swamps. And get there you did.

The house itself, it seemed, offered little but the stench of decaying timber— a scent one would fervently wish to evade. As you cross the platform leading to the mansion's creaking porch, you become aware of the oppressive force that is this house. A visible, uncompromising, and suffocating force. Yet, these thoughts remain speculative for now. After all, the mist enshrouding the house might simply be pollen— a strange, violet mist, heavy with a musty, rotting odor.

The urge to flee nearly overwhelms you. Could it be perilous? Possibly toxic? Or even cursed? Despite these fears, the dire financial burdens and gravely ill loved ones compel a surge of bravery from within. Life, as the saying goes, nudges you to confront the challenge. To advance with determination. To uncover whether there's anything worth salvaging in this accursed estate.

Thus, you find yourself there, turning the dilapidated, corroded door handle, embarking on what may well be the most treacherous venture of your life. All to glean the remnants of glory and wealth that Hexcliff Hall may yet reveal. But truly,

Are you prepared for what lies ahead?
